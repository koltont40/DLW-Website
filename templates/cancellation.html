Service cancellation

We're sorry to see you go. Follow the steps below to wrap up your DIXIELAND WIRELESS LLC service or transition to a new location. Our team will guide you through equipment pickup and final billing.

1. Submit your request

Send us an email at {{ contact_email }} or open a ticket in the client portal. Include your preferred disconnect date and account number.

2. Schedule equipment return

3. Finalize your account

A final invoice is generated once service is disconnected. Pay online through the client portal or contact billing for assistance.
