About DIXIELAND WIRELESS LLC

DixieLand Wireless keeps rural communities, small towns, and growing neighborhoods connected with fast WISP internet and dependable phone service backed by local experts.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves world-class connectivity. Our team designs, deploys, and maintains wireless networks that deliver fiber-like experiences without the wait.

What We Do

  • High-speed fixed wireless coverage across the DixieLand service area
  • Managed phone solutions with full E911 support
  • Professional installation, maintenance, and network monitoring
  • Customer portal for billing, appointments, equipment, and support
